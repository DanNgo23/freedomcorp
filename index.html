Getting Started

This application I created builds a data dashboard for the sales teams of FreedomCorp. FreedomCorp specializes in selling all kinds of wholesale goods to companies across the nation.

They sell raw materials like planks of wood and bags of cement to construction companies, TVs and computers to electronic stores, refrigerators and washing machines to household appliance stores, and a range of furniture to different outlets.

The data dashboard allows company decision-makers to find out which parts of their business are doing better than others. Their sales team is split between four different regions of the U.S. and for each region, the company wants to be able to evaluate regions and compare regional statistics.

All companies FreedomCorp conducts business with is categorized as a small, medium, or large company. Sales teams' performances are tracked over an entire year based on different metrics (call duration, sales generated, units sold).

To explore, click INSTRUCTIONS to officially begin.

Instructions

Click and drag anywhere in the grey timeline (bottom of the page) to only include dates within a specific time period.

Choose a metric from the dropdown list (left of the page) as the method of evaluation.

After choosing a time period and a metric, the stacked area chart, donut chart, and 3 bar charts will all adjust accordingly. Enjoy!
